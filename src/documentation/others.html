Background image hero

Specify the position of a background image:

Background top position:

                
                  <div class="bg-img-hero" style="background-image: url(...); height: 250px;"></div>

Background bottom position:

                
                  <div class="bg-img-hero-bottom" style="background-image: url(...); height: 250px;"></div>

Background center position:

                
                  <div class="bg-img-hero-center" style="background-image: url(...); height: 250px;"></div>

Background fixed position:

                
                  <div class="bg-img-hero-fixed" style="background-image: url(...); height: 250px;"></div>
